# Vision SaaS: Client User Guide

Welcome to Vision! This guide will help you navigate the system based on your assigned role.

---

## 🔑 1. Getting Started
*   **Login**: Access the login page and enter your corporate credentials.
*   **Profile**: Click on your avatar to view your profile, change your password, or logout.
*   **Navigation**: Use the sidebar to navigate between Modules (Tasks, Attendance, Leaves, etc.).

---

## 🛡️ 2. Admin & HR Manager Guide (System Setup)
As an Admin or HR Manager, you are responsible for the organizational foundation.

### Step 1: Configure Your Company
*   Navigate to **Settings > Rules**.
*   Define working hours, weekly offs, and Geofence radius.
*   Set your **Gamification Rules**: How many points for "Critical" vs "Regular" tasks? What are the late penalties?

### Step 2: Onboard Employees
*   Go to **Employees > Add Employee**.
*   Enter basic details, job title, and salary structure.
*   **Hierarchy Assignment**: Assign them a Reporting Manager and an HR Manager. This determines their workflow and visibility.

### Step 3: Global Reports
*   Go to **Reports**.
*   Select the data type (Attendance, Tasks, or Payroll) and date range.
*   Export to **Excel** for deep-dive analysis.

---

## 👑 3. Manager & Assistant Manager Guide (Work Execution)
Your primary focus is managing the output and well-being of your team.

### Step 1: Task Assignment
*   Navigate to **Tasks > Create Task**.
*   Provide a clear work description, select a priority, and set a strict deadline.
*   Assign it to an Employee or Assistant Manager in your hierarchy.

### Step 2: Attendance Auditing
*   Go to **Attendance > Summary**.
*   Monitor real-time check-ins. Review "Flags" (e.g., Outside Geofence, Device Changed) to maintain workforce integrity.

### Step 3: Approvals
*   Check the **Notifications** or sidebar for **Leaves** and **Regularizations**.
*   Review the requests and click **Approve** or **Reject** with comments.

---

## 🧑‍💻 4. Employee Guide (Daily Workflow)
Focus on executing tasks, maintaining attendance, and climbing the leaderboard.

### Step 1: Check-In/Out
*   Upon arrival, click the **Check-In** button in the header.
*   Ensure your browser's location permission is enabled. The system will verify you are within the office Geofence.
*   Don't forget to **Check-Out** before leaving.

### Step 2: Manage Your Tasks
*   Go to **Tasks** to see your active workload.
*   Update status to "In Progress" when you start.
*   Mark as "Completed" as soon as you finish to maximize your **Reward Points**.

### Step 3: Rewards & Leaderboard
*   Check your **Dashboard** to see your total points and efficiency score.
*   Visit the **Leaderboard** to see how you rank against your colleagues.

---

## 🤖 5. Using AI Workforce Intelligence
Vision features a powerful AI engine to assist your daily operations.

*   **AI Dashboard Summary**: Look at the top of your dashboard for a natural language summary of your status or your team's health.
*   **AI Assistant**: Click the floating chat icon in the bottom corner. You can ask:
    *   *"Who is currently overloaded in my team?"*
    *   *"Are there any tasks at risk of missing deadlines?"*
    *   *"What is my productivity score for this month?"*
*   **AI Reports**: Go to **Reports** and select "AI-Enriched Export" for advanced analysis on burnout risks and performance trends.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips
*   **Global Search**: Use the search bar at the top (Cmd/Ctrl + K) to quickly find employees, tasks, or settings.
*   **Dark Mode**: Toggle the theme in your profile settings for a focused evening work experience.
*   **Task Chat**: Use the chat bubble icon on any task to discuss details with your manager without leaving the application.
